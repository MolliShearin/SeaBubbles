Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any off seasons or days closed?

Generally unless bad weather conditions, we are not closed, nor do we have any off seasons, we are open 365 days of the years

In general how long are dives?

Depends on a variety of factors but generally you are looking at a 3 hour experience.

Can children Scuba Dive?

Any one who is 10 or above is allowed to scuba dive, those under 18 are in smaller groups however.

What if I have pre-existing medical conditions can I still dive?

If those medical conditions impact your physical health, such as the heart or the lung, you will be unable to dive.

What if I don't want to dive with my party?

No problem. We offer snorkerling while your group is diving. The boat captain will be supervising.

I don't see prices on this website, how much will it be?

We encourage over the phone or an in-person schedlung due to factors listed above, so that we can give you an accurate price depending on your vacation. TL;DR it's not a flat price.

Will I need to bring anything?

Just bring a swim suit and we will prvoide the rest, you are allowed to bring your own mask if you prefer.

If there are bad weather conditions should I expect a refund.

Of course, we will always accomodate bad weather, just contact us to get the details of your trip in order.

Do I have to sign the waiver in order to dive?

Yes, you will need to sign it. We require everyone who is going diving to sign the waiver before diving

IF you still have more questions or concerns please contact us!
